Welcome to My Portfolio

Hi, I'm Michael, a multimedia artist with an animation, illustration, and game development background. I'm particularly interested in game design and AI artwork and have developed my skills using software like Unity and Unreal Engine. I enjoy writing tabletop RPG rule sets and immersing myself in the world of fantasy through books, games, anime, movies, and television. I'm passionate about my work and am always eager to learn and explore new techniques and concepts. Please take a look at my portfolio to see some of my work.
